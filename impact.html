Our Journey

2018

Foundation

JeevanJal Initiative was founded with a mission to bring clean water to rural Maharashtra. Started with 5 villages.

2020

First Major Milestone

Reached 15 villages and built our 50th well. Established community training programs for sustainable maintenance.

2022

Expansion Phase

Expanded to 25 villages and introduced advanced filtration systems. Partnered with local government initiatives.

2024

Current Impact

Now serving 37 villages with 124 wells built. Over 9,800 lives transformed through sustainable water access.

2025

Future Goals

Aiming to reach 100 villages and serve 25,000 families. Focus on rainwater harvesting and community empowerment.
