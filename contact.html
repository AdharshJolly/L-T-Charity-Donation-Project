Frequently Asked Questions

How can I donate to JeevanJal Initiative?
You can donate through our website, bank transfer, or by contacting us directly. We accept donations of all sizes and provide tax receipts for all contributions.
Can I volunteer with your organization?
Yes! We welcome volunteers with various skills. Whether you're a technical expert, educator, or simply passionate about our cause, there are ways to get involved.
How do you select villages for projects?
We prioritize villages based on water scarcity levels, population size, existing infrastructure, and community readiness to participate in maintenance programs.
Do you provide progress updates on projects?
Absolutely! We publish quarterly reports and send regular updates to donors about project progress, impact metrics, and success stories from the field.
Can organizations partner with JeevanJal?
We welcome partnerships with corporations, NGOs, and government agencies. Contact us to discuss collaboration opportunities that align with our mission.
How do you ensure project sustainability?
We train local communities in system maintenance, establish village water committees, and provide ongoing technical support to ensure long-term success.
